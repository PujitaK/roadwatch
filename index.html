How It Works

From photo to fixed — in days, not years.

A streamlined process that actually delivers results, not just receipts.

01
📸

Snap & Report

Take a photo of any road damage. Our AI instantly identifies the type and severity — no forms to fill.

02
🤖

AI Analyses

Computer vision classifies the damage. GPS tags the location. The right authority is identified automatically.

03
📬

Routed & Tracked

Complaint reaches the correct Executive Engineer. You track every status change in real time.

04
✅

Verified Fixed

When fixed, ANY nearby user verifies with a fresh photo — not just the reporter. 3 community confirmations = officially resolved. No faking it.
